WELCOME TO OUR SCHOOL

Welcome to the website of RPS Public School Kosli. Quality education is the key to nurturing talent, which is crucial for the development of the society. Our goal is to nurture future generations to become citizens who are socially responsible and equipped with a sense of national identity, love for RPS Kosli and International perspectives. We need to create, for students, teachers, principals and parents, a learning and teaching environment that is stable, caring, inspiring and satisfying.
